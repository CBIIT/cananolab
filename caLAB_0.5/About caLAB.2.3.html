caLAB Welcome Workflow Page
Once logged in on the Welcome Login page, caLAB opens to a second Welcome page where you can:
•
Access caLAB Quick Links (hypertext links in the left sidebar of the viewer) that include Quick Links to the National Cancer Institute (NCI) website and the NCI Center for Bioinformatics website.
•
Use menu options pertinent to caLAB. They display along the top of the viewer page: Home, Workflow, Administration, Search, Help, Logout.
•
Review a recommended caLAB workflow. For more information, see Executing a caLAB Workflow.
