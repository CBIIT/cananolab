Composition
When defining Physical Characterization, click the Composition menu tab to enter nanoparticle composition annotations. Characterization by composition is customized for each type of nanoparticle. (Only the Composition form varies from nanoparticle to nanoparticle.) Because you have already selected particle type on the General Information page (see Submitting Annotations for Nanoparticles), the form that opens when you click the Composition link will already be customized for your particle type.
Follow the appropriate links for more information about entering composition information for your nanparticle of interest.
