caNanoLab Functionality Overview
The caNanoLab Home page (see Welcome to caNanoLab Home Page) displays a workflow to follow when using caNanoLab. For ease of use, the workflow corresponds tasks listed under the caNanoLab menu options.
Each step in the graphic is a “hot spot”. Click the hot spot to open the appropriate caNanoLab page for that task.
For specific information about each component of the workflow, see the Related Topics button.
Based on your assigned privileges you may or may not have access to all the options in the workflow. For more information about caNanoLab privileges, see Permissions and Visibility.
During sample annotation, you can submit protocol associations with the sample. With assigned permissions, annotations can be published to multiple user groups such as the public, CCNE researcher, etc. The annotation feature facilitates information sharing of characterizations and assay results outcomes.
