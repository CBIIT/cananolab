Logging into caNanoLab/Updating Password
1.
To log into caNanoLab, from the Welcome to caNanoLab Login Page, enter your login ID and password.
When you register as a new user, you are assigned a temporary password by the administrator. The first time you log in after receiving your account, the Update Password dialog box opens automatically, allowing you to reset your password. At any time, from the login page, you can reconfigure your password by clicking the Update Password button. Enter the appropriate information on the form, and click Update. To clear the form, click the Reset button.
2.
Click Login to enter caNanoLab.
