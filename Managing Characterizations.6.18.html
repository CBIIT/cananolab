Adding Data and Conditions
In the Data and Conditions segment of the window, you can create a matrix where you can enter data values and/or other information such as laboratory conditions that are part of your findings.
To define the matrix for the data, follow these steps.
Click [Other] to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, your entry is enclosed in [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear.
1.
To define the matrix size, enter numbers for rows and columns and click the Update button.
The window now displays coumn [#} headings that you can name for your matrix.
2.
Click the underlined column heading(s). Available options can be specific for the type of characteristic you are defining. As described below, select an option or if there are none or if you prefer, select [Other] to name the column yourself.
a.
Column Type – You must select either Datum or Condition. In the cases noted below, based on your Characterization selection at the top of the Characerization page, customized options may display for the following fields.
b.
Column Name and Column Property – Select or enter a column name.

3.
Click Set Column Order to indicate the order of the column headings in the matrix you have defined.
4.
Click Save to confirm the your matrix design choices. After you do so, the names you selected show as column headings. Click Remove to delete the column, or Cancel to close the window.
5.
Click Save in the Finding window, as well.
After adding data and conditions to the sample, continue the process described in Adding an In Vitro Characterization.
