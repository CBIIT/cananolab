Toxicity
Toxicity refers to the potential of nanoparticles to be harmful to cell health or viabilty. Because toxicity is ascertained under laboratory conditions, it is one of the in vitro characterizations in caNanoLab.
Once you click the Add link corresponding to Toxicity, two menu bar options become available, Oxidative Stress and Enzyme Function. On the Particle Tree, the Toxicity category also includes subcategories Cytotoxicity and Immunotoxicity, both of which have sub-categories.
Click either of the tabs to open the corresponding form. For more information about completing a form, see In Vitro Characterization Forms.
