Adding a Molecular Weight Physical Characterization
In caNanoLab Nanoparticle Characterization is defined with Physical and In Vitro Characterizations.
Molecular Weight is a Physical Characterization.
In the Navigation Tree the Add button next to the Molecular Weight name enables you to add physical characterizations to caNanoLab.
If you do not have certain security privileges, the Add buttons will be hidden, and you will have read-only access to the existing items.
To record molecular weight data for a particle, follow these steps:
1.
On the Navigation Tree next to the Molecular Weight heading click the Add button. The Physical Characterization-Molecular Weight page opens.
2.
Complete the following fields on the form.
Some fields may contain the Other option. Click Other to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, yourr entry is sandwiched by [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear.

Copy to Other Nanoparticle Samples
This option copies files and summary information to one or more selected particles from the same vendor. Select one or more particle(s) to which the copied data is to be transferred. (Only appropriate particles are listed.) For more information, see Copying Characterizations.

When you have finished, click Submit or click Reset to clear all fields on the form.
In the Navigation Tree, a hypertext link for the new characterization is added under Molecular Weight.
