Toxicity Category
Each subcategory of In Vitro Characterization categories is listed under the toxicity heading. Toxicity refers to the potential of nanoparticles to be harmful to cell health or viabilty; toxicity is ascertained under laboratory conditions.
Toxicity subcategories include the following list, each of which is broken down further by subheadings described in these topics:
