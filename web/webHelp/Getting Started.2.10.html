Welcome to caNanoLab Home Page
Once you log into caNanoLab, the Welcome to caNanoLab Home page opens. The flowchart on the Home page illustrates a general workflow to use in caNanoLab. For more information about this workflow diagram, see caNanoLab Functionality Overview.
The caNanoLab workspace comprises the menu bar and the blue panel on the left side of the browser.
•
All users can access caNanoLab Quick Links (hypertext links in the blue panel on the left sidebar of the browser). These links to the National Cancer Institute (NCI) website, the NCI Center for Bioinformatics website, and the Nanotechnology Characterization Laboratory (NCL) website.
•
The caNanoLab menu options are displayed according to assigned permissions. See Permissions and Visibility. Options which may be available are described as follows:
Some fields may contain the [Other] option. Click [Other] to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, your entry is enclosed in [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear.

When you add a sample to caNanoLab with the Samples option, options for adding annotations and publications to the sample become available in the left sidebar, called the Navigation Tree. For more information about the Navigation Tree, see Understanding the Navigation Tree.
Publications
Submit new and search for existing sample publications. For more information, see Managing Publications.
