Deleting a Document
From an open nanoparticle, you can add or delete associated documents. To delete a document, follow these steps:
1.
Click the Documents section in the Navigation Tree. A list appears, showing any documents associated with the open particle.
2.
Click the Delete button. The Delete page opens, showing the same document list.
3.
To clear the fields and start again, click the Reset button
4.
Insert a check by any document you want to delete, and click the Delete button.
