Adding Sample Composition Annotations
To add composition properties, click the Sample Composition link. Click the Add button corresponding to a Composition subcategory.
Composition is customized for each nanoparticle type. The first item that you will select for Nanoparticle Entity, the particle type, will determine the composition fields as you proceed to annotate the nanoparticle sample.
