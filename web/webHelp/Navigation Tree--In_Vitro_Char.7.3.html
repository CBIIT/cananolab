In Vitro Characterization Overview
Nanoparticle characterization, describing distinctive characteristics or essential features of the nanoparticle determined through analytical methods, records information associated with nanoparticle synthesis and properties. Nanoparticles can be characterized in caNanoLab by physical characteristics and by in vitro characteristics. In vitro characterization is the determination of the effect of nanoparticles on living cells in an artificial laboratory environment outside of the living organism.
