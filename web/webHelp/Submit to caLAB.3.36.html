Deleting a Characterization
You can delete a characterization for a selected particle in one of two ways:
1.
From the Particle Tree open for a nanoparticle, each characterization category to which you can add or delete annotations displays a hypertext delete to its right on the Particle Tree.
a.
Click the Delete link. The Delete Characterizations page that opens displays a list of existing characterizations for the nanoparticle.
b.
Select each characterization you want to delete.
c.
Click the Delete button at the bottom of the page or click Reset to clear the selections.
2.
From the Particle Tree open for a nanoparticle, existing annotations are listed as hypertext links under Characterizations/Physical.
a.
Click a link, opening the nanoparticle characterization form that displays the annotations.
b.
At the bottom left corner of the form, click the Delete button.
This only deletes the characterization(s) for the selected particle. If it (they) has (have) been copied for other particles, it won't be deleted.
Deleted characterizations are placed in the particle archive for history purposes.
