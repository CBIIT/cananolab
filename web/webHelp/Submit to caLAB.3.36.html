Deleting a Characterization
To delete a characterization for a selected particle, open the nanoparticle characterization form that displays the characterization. At the bottom left corner of the form, click the Delete button. When this is done, the characterization is placed in the particle archive for history purposes.
This only deletes the characterization for the selected particle. If it has been copied for other particles, it won't be deleted.
