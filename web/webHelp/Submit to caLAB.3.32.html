Immunotoxicity
Immunotoxicity is a subcategory under the In Vitro characterization, Toxicity. Immunotoxicity has two subcategories, Blood Contact and Immune Cell Function, both of which have subcategories.
Blood Contact
Once you click the Add link corresponding to Blood Contact, four menu bar options become available, Plate Aggregation, Hemolysis, Plasma Protein Binding and Coagulation.
Click any of these tabs to open the corresponding form. For more information about completing a form, see In Vitro Characterization Forms.
Immune Cell Function
Once you click the Add link corresponding to Immune Cell Function, eight menu bar options become available: Oxidative Burst, Chemotaxis, Leukocyte Proliferation, Phagocytosis, Cytokine Induction, CFU_GM, Complement Activation, Cytotoxic Activity of NK Cells.
Click any of these tabs to open the corresponding form. For more information about completing a form, see In Vitro Characterization Forms.
