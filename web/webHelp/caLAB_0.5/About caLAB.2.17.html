Assay Run In and Out Folders
Within a workflow, when you click consecutively on (+) symbols to open an assay and an associated run, if the In or Out folder for the run has a (+) symbol, it is populated with either aliquots or input or output files or both. Click the In or Out text itself. At that point, several hypertext links become available in the right-hand pane. Click these hypertext links for information about each link.
