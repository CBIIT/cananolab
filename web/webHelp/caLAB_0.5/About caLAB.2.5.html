caLAB Online Help
Online Help has been written to help you master the caLAB software.
To access Online Help, use either the following methods:
•
Select the Help menu option. That opens online help displaying an introductory topic and a table of contents that you can browse to locate your topic of interest. You can also use the online help index or use the search feature to locate a par­ticular topic.
•
Click the Help button in any viewer window. Accessed this way, online help opens to a page-sensitive topic.
The “breadcrumb” trail at the top of the online help screen forms a linked path to show you your location in the online content. You can click the steps in the path to step through the topics that led to the topic being viewed.
