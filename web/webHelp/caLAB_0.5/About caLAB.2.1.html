Introduction to caLAB
Welcome to the cancer Laboratory Analysis Bench (caLAB). caLAB is a Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) designed to capture the workflow in a labora­tory. caLAB provides support for the execution of assays and the recording of assay results. Additionally, caLAB allows for the management of laboratory inventory (sam­ples) that is leveraged as input to laboratory assays.
caLAB 0.5 will be accessible only to users at the Nanotechnology Characterization Lab­oratory (NCL). caLAB source code is available at the National Cancer Institute Center for Bioinformatics (NCICB) download center: http://ncicb.nci.nih.gov/download/.
