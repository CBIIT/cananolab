Masking an Aliquot
Once you have assigned an aliquot to an Assay Run, it is listed in the In folder of the run, along with other aliquots or input files you may have uploaded for the run. In caLAB 1.0caLAB 1.0, you cannot delete objects from the system, but you can mask them. As an example reason for doing so, perhaps you created an aliquot erroneously, or you learned an aliquot was contaminated and you do not want it to be recognized as an active component in an assay.
To mask an aliquot, follow these steps:
1.
Select an Assay Run in the workflow tree.
2.
Click on the (+) symbol to open the In folder of your selected Run. Each item listed in the folder is a hyperlink that can open its corresponding details.
3.
Click on the listed aliquot to view its properties.
4.
Click the Mask Aliquot button at the bottom right of the browser.
The Mask Aliquot dialog box that opens asks you to confirm that you want to mask the specified aliquot. In addition, you must enter the reason you are masking the aliquot.
5.
Enter the reason for the mask, and click Yes to confirm the mask.
A masked aliquot does not disappear from the workflow tree, but its name becomes italicized when it is masked. You can still view the aliquot details by clicking on it, but the Mask Aliquot button is no longer present on the details page.
