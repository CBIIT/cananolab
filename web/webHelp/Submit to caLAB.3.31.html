Cytotoxicity
Cytotoxicity is a subcategory under the In Vitro characterization, Toxicity. Cytotoxicity examines the cell destruction and/or death caused by nanoparticles. It can be dependent upon the nanoparticle itself, the agent and dosage it carries, the target cell type, etc.
Once you click the Add link corresponding to Cytotoxicity, two menu bar options become available, Cell Viabiltiy and Caspase 3 Activity.
