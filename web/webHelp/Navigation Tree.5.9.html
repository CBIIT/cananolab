Physical Characterization Category
Nanoparticle characterization, describing distinctive characteristics or essential features of the nanoparticle determined through analytical methods, is necessary to record information associated with nanoparticle synthesis and properties. Nanoparticles can be characterized in caNanoLab 1.4 by physical characteristics and by in vitro characteristics. Physical characterization refers to the determination of the material and structural properties of a nanoparticle.
When you click the Navigation Tree Physical Characterizations button, subcategories expand below the heading. (Click the button again to collapse the subcategories). If characterization information is already filled out for the nanoparticle, hyperlinked characterizations are listed under each subcategory. You may have the option to add or delete details with the Add and Delete button.
If you do not have certain security privileges, the Add and Delete buttons will be hidden, and you will have read-only access to the existing items.
