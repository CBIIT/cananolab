Chemical Association
This Sample Composition subcategory allows you to enter basic annotation information describing the chemical association(s) of the nanoparticle/functionalizing entities of the nanoparticle sample.
To enter chemical association information, you must have entered at least two composing elements and/or at least one functionalizing entity for the nanoparticle entity.
1.
Once you have entered nanoparticle and functionalizing entity information, click the Chemical Association Add button in the left sidebar.
2.
Fill in each of the following fields on the Chemical Association composition form Asterisks indicate required fields.
Some fields may contain the Other option. Click Other to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, your entry is enclosed in [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear.
