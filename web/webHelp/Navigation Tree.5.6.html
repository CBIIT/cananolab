Functionalizing Entity
This Sample Composition subcategory allows you to enter basic annotation information for the functional element of the nanoparticle sample. Each Functionalizing Entity form starts with a summary section that names the functionalizing entity type. The first item that you select for Functionalizing Entity will determine the composition fields that appear on the form.
To complete the form, select the entity type, and continue as follows:
For Antibody, click the arrow for further instructions
1.
Fill in each of the following fields on the Antibody composition form:
Some fields may contain the Other option. Click Other to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, your entry is sandwiched by [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear.
