Submitting/Updating a Report
Update Report: If you perform a report search, on the Nanoparticle Reports Search Results page, and click the hypertext Report Title in the first column, this Update Report page opens. On this page, you can review and/or edit the information about the report that was defined when it was submitted. See Report Update Page for more information.
Submit Report: This feature allows you to upload a final nanoparticle report for a vendor or researcher. The report can contain information about multiple particles.
To upload a nanoparticle report, follow these steps:
1.
Click the Reports caNanoLab menu option. On the Manage Report Home page, click the Submit a New Report link. This opens the Submit Nanoparticle Report form.
2.
Select or enter the appropriate information in the text fields and drop-down lists. Fields are described in the following table. Asterisks indicate required fields.
3.
On the Submit Nanoparticle Report form that opens, select or complete the following fields.
Some fields may contain the Other option. Click Other to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, your entry is enclosed in [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear
