Submitting a Protocol
You can access this page after logging into caNanoLab or you can access publicly available nanoparticle protocols from the Login page.
To create a new protocol, follow these steps:
1.
Click the Protocols caNanoLab menu option. On the Manage Protocols Home page, click the Submit a New Protocol link. This opens the Submit Protocol form.
2.
Select or enter the appropriate information in the text fields and drop-down lists. Fields are described in the following table. Items with an asterisk are required fields.
Some fields may contain the Other option. Click Other to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, your entry is sandwiched by [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear.
