In Vitro Characterization
For an overview of nanoparticle characterization, see Nanoparticle Characterization.
The In Vitro characterization category on the Particle Tree includes several categories where you can annotate nanoparticles with laboratory results obtained under in vitro conditions. Click Add to the right of any the following categories. This opens corresponding menu bars of a type with hypertext labels that you click to open a form where you can add corresponding information.
Toxicity
Immunotoxicity
Cytotoxicity
Immune Cell Function
Most of the fields on the forms that open for these in vitro characterizaÂ­tion categories are identical. They differ only when you click Number of Histogram/Bar Charts, enter the number and click Update. At that point, the page extends, and for different characterizations, the value names are different, as noted in the tables described in the topics.
For more information about completing any of the forms, see In Vitro Characterization Forms.
