Navigation in caLAB
After clicking menu options across the top of the caLAB browser window, the application advances to corresponding pages. Additionally, after you have executed a workflow (under the Workflow menu option), the workflow displays in the left sidebar.
The workflow in caLAB 1.0 displays four assay types, two of which are populated with data in caLAB 1.0: Pre-screening and In Vitro. Each item in the workflow for which information currently exists is preceded by a (+) and a “folder” icon. (Items with no current information display a “piece of paper” icon. Click on the (+) icon to expand the folders.
Many text items in caLAB are hypertext links that you can click to open detail pages, dialog boxes, or browser windows with more hypertext links for accessing features. Most pages also display Help buttons that you can click to open page sensitive online help.
For more information about working with a workflow, see Executing a caLAB Workflow.
