Defining In Vivo Design and Methods
In the Design and Methods sections, complete the fields as described in the following table.
Click [Other] to enter an alternative to the selections listed. At that point, your entry is enclosed in [new entry] brackets, indicating that your entry is temporary. Once you click Submit (or Update), your entry is persisted and the brackets disappear.

When you select a technique, the Abbreviation field may populate automatically if an abbreviation is known. If not, you can enter your own abbreviation.

Instrument
Click Add to expand the page where you can enter or select identifying information about the instrument used to obtain data.
Manufacturer – Select the instrument manufacturer.
Model Name – Enter the model name.
Type – Leave blank or click [Other] to open a text box where you can enter a new instrument type. Click Add to do so or Cancel to close the window.
Once you have specified the data, click Save to save the information to the sample or Cancel to close the mini-window without saving data.
